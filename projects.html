JARVIS 1.1 - AI ASSISTANT PYTHON

JARVIS 1.1 is a Python-based AI assistant designed to simplify everyday tasks and introduce users to the world of automation. This version of JARVIS can perform a range of functions, including checking the time, opening websites, solving mathematical problems, sending emails, and more. As a beginner-friendly project, it serves as a stepping stone for those new to programming, helping to explore Python and the fundamentals of AI development. With a focus on usability and learning, JARVIS 1.1 is not just a tool but a foundation for building more advanced and feature-rich versions in the future.
